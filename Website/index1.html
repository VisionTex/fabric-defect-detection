Hole Image

Hole

An opening or tear in the fabric where warp and weft threads are missing.

Grade C

Slub

A thick spot in yarn resulting in an uneven appearance in the fabric.

Grade B

Oil Stain

Thread Break

Fabric Grading System

VisionTex implements both the American Grading System and a CAT (Categorized Attributes of Textiles) points system for comprehensive defect evaluation:

American Grading System

Grade Description Quality Level
Grade A Minor defects with minimal impact on quality Premium Quality
Grade B Moderate defects with some impact on appearance Standard Quality
Grade C Major defects affecting functionality or appearance Secondary Quality
Grade D Severe defects making fabric unsuitable for primary use Rejected

CAT Points System

Points Defect Severity Impact on Fabric Value
1 Point Minor defect (barely noticeable) 0-5% reduction
2 Points Noticeable defect (visible on close inspection) 5-15% reduction
3 Points Significant defect (readily visible) 15-30% reduction
4 Points Major defect (severely affecting fabric quality) 30-50% reduction
5 Points Critical defect (renders fabric unusable for intended purpose) >50% reduction

Our system calculates total points per unit area to determine overall fabric quality and pricing recommendations.
